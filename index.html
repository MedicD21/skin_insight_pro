Privacy Policy

This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using SkinInsightPro, you agree to these practices.

What we collect

  • Account data (email, name, role, company association).
  • Client records you create (names, contact details, notes, medical history, allergies, sensitivities, medications, and products to avoid).
  • Photos and analysis results you upload or generate.
  • Consent records (signature and consent date).
  • App diagnostics (error logs, performance data).
  • HIPAA audit logs (user activity, PHI access, timestamps, device info).
  • Session activity data (for automatic 15-minute timeout).
  • Subscription and purchase data (managed through Apple App Store).

Data we do not collect

  • Precise location data.
  • Contacts from your device address book.
  • Biometric data (Face ID/Touch ID is handled entirely by iOS and never leaves your device).
  • Payment card data (payments are processed securely through Apple's App Store).
  • Advertising identifiers for tracking.

How we use data

  • Provide skin analysis results and history.
  • Manage client profiles and team access within a company.
  • Process and manage subscriptions for premium features.
  • Enforce automatic session timeout after 15 minutes of inactivity (HIPAA compliance).
  • Track all access to protected health information in audit logs (HIPAA compliance).
  • Improve app reliability and troubleshoot issues.
  • Comply with legal requirements (HIPAA audit logging, consent tracking, breach notification).

AI analysis processing

Skin analysis can be performed using on-device Apple Vision or a cloud AI provider (Claude), depending on your settings. When a cloud provider is selected, images and related context are sent to the provider to generate analysis results. We do not use your data to train our models.

In-app purchases and subscriptions

SkinInsightPro operates on a freemium model with monthly or annual subscriptions managed through Apple's App Store. Free tier users receive 5 Apple Vision analyses per month. Subscribers gain unlimited access to advanced Claude AI analysis and additional premium features. All payments are processed securely by Appleâ€”we never see or store your payment information.

Sharing and disclosure

We do not sell your data. We only share data with service providers required to operate the app (such as hosting, database, or AI analysis when selected) or when required by law.

Data storage and retention

Data is stored in our backend services for as long as your account remains active or as needed to provide the service. You can request deletion of your account or specific data.

Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission.
  • Optional Face ID/Touch ID authentication for app access.
  • Automatic session timeout after 15 minutes of inactivity.
  • HIPAA audit logging of all PHI access events.
  • Secure cloud storage with access controls and encryption at rest.

No system is 100% secure, but we continuously improve our safeguards.

HIPAA notice

SkinInsightPro includes HIPAA-compliant features designed for healthcare professionals:

  • Audit Logging: All access to client records (PHI) is logged with timestamps, user identity, and device information.
  • Session Controls: Automatic timeout after 15 minutes of inactivity to prevent unauthorized access.
  • Consent Management: Digital signature capture and consent tracking for each client.
  • Access Controls: Role-based permissions and company-level data separation.
  • Data Export: Ability to export audit logs and client data for compliance documentation.

Important: While SkinInsightPro provides HIPAA-compliant tools and security features, you (the healthcare provider or covered entity) are ultimately responsible for ensuring your use of the app complies with HIPAA regulations. This includes signing a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) with your cloud service providers (Supabase, Anthropic) if you handle PHI. We recommend consulting with a HIPAA compliance expert to ensure your specific use case meets all requirements.

Your choices and rights

  • Update or correct your information in the app.
  • Enable or disable Face ID/Touch ID authentication.
  • Choose between on-device (Apple Vision) or cloud-based (Claude AI) skin analysis.
  • Export your data including client records and audit logs.
  • Request account deletion and complete data removal.
  • Manage subscription status through the Apple App Store.
  • Contact support for access, correction, or deletion requests.

Children

SkinInsightPro is not intended for use by children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy over time. The latest version will always be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
