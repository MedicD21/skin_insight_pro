Privacy Policy

This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using SkinInsightPro, you agree to these practices.

What we collect

  • Account data (email, name, role, company association).
  • Client records you create (names, contact details, notes, medical history, allergies, sensitivities, medications, and products to avoid).
  • Photos and analysis results you upload or generate.
  • Consent records (signature and consent date).
  • App diagnostics (error logs, performance data).

Data we do not collect

  • Precise location data.
  • Contacts from your device address book.
  • Payment card data (payments are not processed in-app).
  • Advertising identifiers for tracking.

How we use data

  • Provide skin analysis results and history.
  • Manage client profiles and team access within a company.
  • Improve app reliability and troubleshoot issues.
  • Comply with legal requirements (HIPAA-related workflows and consent tracking).

AI analysis processing

Skin analysis can be performed using on-device Apple Vision or a cloud AI provider (Claude), depending on your settings. When a cloud provider is selected, images and related context are sent to the provider to generate analysis results. We do not use your data to train our models.

Sharing and disclosure

We do not sell your data. We only share data with service providers required to operate the app (such as hosting, database, or AI analysis when selected) or when required by law.

Data storage and retention

Data is stored in our backend services for as long as your account remains active or as needed to provide the service. You can request deletion of your account or specific data.

Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. No system is 100% secure, but we continuously improve our safeguards.

HIPAA notice

SkinInsightPro supports HIPAA-related workflows (such as consent capture and access controls), but SkinInsightPro is not a covered entity or business associate by default. Customers are responsible for ensuring their own HIPAA compliance and for using the app in a compliant manner.

Your choices and rights

  • Update or correct your information in the app.
  • Request account deletion and data removal.
  • Contact support for access or deletion requests.

Children

SkinInsightPro is not intended for use by children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy over time. The latest version will always be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Support

If you need help, contact us at support_skininsightpro@proton.me.

When contacting support, include:

  • The email used for your account.
  • Your device model and iOS version.
  • A brief description of the issue and steps to reproduce.
  • Screenshots or screen recordings if available.

We typically respond within 1-2 business days.
